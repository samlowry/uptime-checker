Sponsors

With gratitude to our sponsor venues, we present a comprehensive exploration of Swingers Clubs across the United States. From the vibrant scenes of Alabama (AL) to the picturesque allure of Alaska (AK), the warmth of Arizona (AZ), and the southern charm of Arkansas (AR). Experience the sophistication of California (CA), the natural beauty of Colorado (CO), and the quaint elegance of Connecticut (CT).

Discover the intimate venues in Delaware (DE) and the cosmopolitan allure of District of Columbia (DC). Revel in the vibrant nightlife of Florida (FL), the southern sophistication of Georgia (GA), and the exotic charm of Hawaii (HI). From the rustic appeal of Idaho (ID) to the urban vibrancy of Illinois (IL) and the welcoming spirit of Indiana (IN).

Explore the heartland charm of Iowa (IA) and Kansas (KS), the rolling hills of Kentucky (KY), and the lively culture of Louisiana (LA). Whether visiting the rugged beauty of Maine (ME) or the historic sites of Maryland (MD), Massachusetts (MA) awaits with its colonial elegance. Enjoy the dynamic energy of Michigan (MI) or the scenic lakes of Minnesota (MN).

Journey through the southern landscapes of Mississippi (MS) and the thriving communities of Missouri (MO), or head west to the wide-open spaces of Montana (MT), Nebraska (NE), and Nevada (NV). New England charm shines in New Hampshire (NH) and New Jersey (NJ), while New Mexico (NM) offers southwestern enchantment.

The vibrant cities of New York (NY), North Carolina (NC), and North Dakota (ND) are just a click away. Don’t miss the cultural gems of Ohio (OH), Oklahoma (OK), and Oregon (OR). The rich history of Pennsylvania (PA) and Rhode Island (RI) awaits.

Find southern charm in South Carolina (SC), explore the plains of South Dakota (SD), and revel in the lively scenes of Tennessee (TN) and Texas (TX). The rugged beauty of Utah (UT) and Vermont (VT) is matched by the charm of Virginia (VA) and the breathtaking views of Washington (WA).

Discover the rich landscapes of West Virginia (WV), Wisconsin (WI), and the frontier spirit of Wyoming (WY).

Web tools and Projects we developed

  • Open-NEM

    The live tracker of the Australian electricity market.

  • Paris Equity Check

    This website is based on a Nature Climate Change study that compares Nationally Determined Contributions with equitable national emissions trajectories in line with the five categories of equity outlined by the IPCC.

  • liveMAGICC Climate Model

    Run one of the most popular reduced-complexity climate carbon cycle models online. Used by IPCC, UNEP GAP reports and numerous scientific publications.

  • NDC & INDC Factsheets

    Check out our analysis of all the post-2020 targets that countries announced under the Paris Agreement.
